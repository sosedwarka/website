High-End 21st Century Skills

The school was established in Sept 2021 with a vision to teach High End â€“ 21st Century skills to students. Apart from regular foundational subjects, students are also taught -Robotics, Digital Media Design, Fashion, Mechatronics, Coding, and Finance.

After taster courses of all six skills in IX and X, students opt for any one skill in XI and XII for specialization. Each skill is taught by a specialized resource person who focuses on the development of our students' personalities while nurturing them and providing global context. Each lesson has a Key and related concept, global context, and Statement of inquiry for deeper understanding. .

The school is in the process of getting affiliated with IB(International Baccalaureate) program and has achieved 90% of the targets. The evaluation visit is expected towards the end of this year. In just three years the school has adopted the philosophy of inquiry, action, and reflection in the global context of education.

Maintaining academic integrity is the school's primary focus, and all students have taken a non-plagiarism pledge. The school also organizes poster-making activities, group discussions, and skits where students are taught to give credit and write proper citations.

Our education system needs to prepare our students for a world which is constantly evolving and growing. The Delhi government has established Dr. B. R. Ambedkar Schools of Specialised Excellence to provide an education which is relevant to these ever-changing needs of the 21st Century. These schools are hallmark of an education system which keeps the learning interests and requirements of our students at the centre and prepares them for the dynamic world, outside the school system.
